## hackAstone 赛事详情（来源：<http://hackastone.varteledu.com>）

### CALL FOR PARTICIPATION

#### Why Join us? “HackATHON Through CAPstone”

**Global Event**

Any tertiary student matriculated in any higher learning institution in the world can register for the contest. Institutions with credit-bearing CAPstone projects are welcome to join this global event.

**Scope**

Participants are required to build AI assistants for higher education, K-12 education, preschool & special needs education, vocational & professional education, lifelong learning, etc.

**Role of Participating Universities**

Each participating university can recommend a maximum of three teams to advance to the semi-finals. The university advisor is an authorised person to make the nomination.

**International Collaboration**

Two universities from different cities, different countries, or even different continents can form a joint team to participate in the contest. Interdisciplinary teaming is also encouraged.

### Theme: “Agentic AI for Education”

**Registration**

- The registration system is open till **28 Feb 2026 23:59 CET**.
- Max 8 qualified students from 1 or 2 universities can form a team to participate in the contest.
- Registered teams will receive an official certificate from the organizer after completing the hackAstone projects.
- To register: [click here](https://hackastone.varteledu.com/hackastone-2026-registration/). Registration is free of charge.

**Project Submission**

- Each participating university can nominate up to three teams for the semi-final phase of the contest **due on 1st June, 2026 (23:59 CET)**.
- Nominated teams have to submit their project info, including a project video (8 mins max) and project description in a given form **before 1 July 2026 (23:59 CET)**.

**Finalist Announcement**

- Finalists will be announced on **1 Aug 2026 (23:59 CET)**.
- Finalists will be invited to present and demonstrate their work at the **2026 Conference on AI for Education (Amsterdam, 29-30 Oct, 2026)**.

**Awards and Winner Announcement**

- Winners will be announced at the **2026 Holland Conference on AI for Education (Amsterdam, 30 Oct 2026)**.
- Finalists will have a chance to win:
  - The HackaStone Gold Award
  - The HackaStone Silver Award
  - The HackaStone Bronze Award
  - The HackaStone Best Collaboration Team Award

*This event is organised by the Association of Global AI Technomics Education & Exchange.*
